Kaushal Ramalingam

Project: MIT App Inventor
Course: Computer Science Principles
Date: October 2018

Our objective for this project was to create a modernized and more user-friendly Android app version of the game Space Invaders. Edward and I mapped out a tier system to tackle the priorites of different features in our app. Lack of familiarity with the interface made it difficult for us to articulate our ideas in code, making this issue one of the most challenging in the development process. I felt that Edward and I worked well together, as were able to productively reach our goals while sharing an equal workload, with Edward focusing more on the technical aspects of the game, while I focused on app layout and documentation. For the development of our app, we initially coded our tutorial then brainstormed all the modifications that we could add to make the game for challenging and user-friendly, organizing these ideas into our tier system. After getting feedback on these ideas from surrounding groups, we evaluated the input and accordingly added them into our tier system as well as decided on our final changes.
